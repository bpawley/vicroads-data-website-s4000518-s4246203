New Facts Panel

1 condition exceeds the average of 24,119 accidents per category.

Clear is the highest-risk condition, with 150,593 accidents — 524.4% above average.

The remaining 7 conditions all fall below the average baseline, with Snowing having the fewest accidents (69).

Note: The high count for "Clear" reflects that most driving happens in clear weather, not necessarily that clear weather is more dangerous per trip than adverse conditions.
